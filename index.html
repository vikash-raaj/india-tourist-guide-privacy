India Tourist Guide

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026

India Tourist Guide ("we", "our", or "the app") is a travel reference app that helps users explore tourist destinations across India. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide core app features:

  • Location data — with your permission, the app accesses your device's precise or approximate location solely to display your position on the in-app map (powered by Google Maps). We do not store, transmit, or share your location with any server or third party beyond what Google Maps requires to render the map.
  • Favorites — tourist spots you mark as favourites are saved locally on your device only using Android's on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To show your current location on the map within the app.
  • To remember tourist spots you have saved as favourites across sessions.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the app's features.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Maps SDK for Android to render interactive maps. When the map is displayed, Google may receive your approximate device location and standard network data (e.g. IP address) per their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy .

We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, or social login providers.

4. Data Storage & Security

All user-generated data (favourites) is stored exclusively on your local device. We operate no servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that receive or store your personal information.

5. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, retention is entirely under your control:

  • Favourites — retained on your device until you clear the app's data, uninstall the app, or manually remove individual favourites within the app.
  • Location data — never stored. Your location is accessed only in real time while the map is active and is discarded immediately after use.

We retain no data on any external server because no data is ever transmitted to us.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored app data at any time using either of these methods:

  • Within the app — open any saved spot and tap the heart / bookmark icon to remove it from your favourites.
  • Via Android Settings — go to Settings → Apps → India Tourist Guide → Storage → Clear Data. This permanently removes all favourites and resets the app to its default state.
  • Uninstall the app — uninstalling India Tourist Guide removes all locally stored data from your device.

To request deletion of any data held by Google Maps (e.g. location history), please refer to Google's Privacy Policy .

7. Permissions We Request

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — to show your position on the map. You may deny this permission; the app will still work without it, but the "show my location" feature will be unavailable.
  • INTERNET — required to load map tiles from Google Maps.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — requested by the Expo framework for potential asset caching; the app does not read or write any personal files.

8. Children's Privacy

India Tourist Guide is a general-audience app and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
